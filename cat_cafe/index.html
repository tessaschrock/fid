Who we are:

Cuppa Cat strives to give cats and kittens temporary foster homes, enriching their lives and giving them the chance to find a human family! We work with our local animal shelters to give patrons an adorable experience, and possibly a new family member!

The main goal is always adoption, however we are happy to provide a relaxing environment for all and we appreciate any and all patronage.

Thinking of stopping by? We highly recommend booking in advance!
